ICB TRACKING SYSTEM

ICB Stands For Intelligent College Bus Tracking System. The ICB Tracking System is an advanced solution designed to provide real-time tracking of college buses. This system enhances the safety and convenience of students, parents, and college authorities by offering live location updates, route optimization, and instant notifications. The app is built with modern GPS technology to ensure accurate tracking and smooth operation. Users can easily access bus schedules, receive alerts about arrival times, and ensure a safe commuting experience.

Essentials: The app provides secure login, user-friendly interface, and real-time notifications for a seamless experience.
