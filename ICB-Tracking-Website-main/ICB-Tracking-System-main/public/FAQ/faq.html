Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the College Bus Tracking System?

Our system allows parents and students to track the location of their bus in real time.

2. How does the tracking work?

We use GPS technology to provide live updates on the bus's location.

3. Is my data safe?

Yes, we follow strict security protocols to protect your data.

4. What should I do if the bus is delayed?

Check the app for real-time updates or contact the school administrator.

5. Can I track multiple buses at the same time?

Yes, if you have multiple children in different buses, you can track them all in one app.

6. Does the app work on all smartphones?

Yes, our app is compatible with both Android and iOS devices.

7. What happens if my GPS is turned off?

To track the bus, GPS must be enabled on your device. Ensure it is turned on for accurate updates.
